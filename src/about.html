About me

Developer, motorsport spectator

Profile

I am a full-stack software engineer based in Austin, Texas. I am passionate about the art of software development and strive to build clean APIs and clear abstractions.

I am skilled in dynamic languages (JavaScript, Python) but learned to program in statically typed languages (Java, C/C++) at Univeristy College London. After getting my BSc. in Computer Science, I spent some time at The University of Texas at Austin researching network and operating system security.

Programming language design and the tradeoffs between safety, performance and developer productivity are my current extracurricular interests. Watching the trajectories of Rust, Swift and Go along with the movement back towards static typing excites me as a developer.

In my spare time, I enjoy watching motorsport (especially endurance racing), circular discussions about the golden age of televsion and adding to—while less frequently subtracting from—my reading list.
